Submission Guidelines for Authors

General Instructions

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished research papers, review articles, and case studies in the fields of technology, management, and interdisciplinary research. Submissions must not be under consideration elsewhere.

Manuscript Format

  • Language: English
  • Font: Times New Roman, Size 12
  • Line spacing: 1.5
  • Margins: 1 inch
  • Length: 6–12 pages

Structure of the Paper

  • Title and Author Details
  • Abstract (150–250 words)
  • Keywords
  • Introduction
  • Literature Review
  • Methodology
  • Results and Discussion
  • Conclusion
  • References

Figures and Tables

All figures and tables should be properly labeled and numbered. Use clear captions and high-quality images.

Referencing Style

Authors must follow APA or IEEE referencing style consistently throughout the manuscript.

Plagiarism Policy

The similarity index should not exceed 10–15%. Papers with high plagiarism will be rejected.

Submission Process

Authors can submit manuscripts via email or through the online submission form.

Email: nijtm@nshm.com

Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo a double-blind peer review process. Authors will be notified within 1–2 weeks.

  • December Issue Deadline: September 30
